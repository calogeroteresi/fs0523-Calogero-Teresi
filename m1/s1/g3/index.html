Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and cremt brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoos salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together ali the dough ingredients unti! the flour is fully absorbed.
